Scandia Åkeri AB

Efficient, Safe, and Reliable Road Freight Logistics.

The Engine of Our Supply Chain

Scandia Åkeri AB is the dedicated logistics and transportation arm of Shamsi General Trading. Established to ensure our products and those of our partners are delivered with punctuality and care, we are the vital link that connects producers with the market. We specialize in food-grade handling and operate with an unwavering commitment to professionalism and safety across Sweden.

Our Core Services

Refrigerated & Food-Grade Transport

We specialize in the transportation of dairy, produce, and other perishable goods, using clean, modern, and temperature-controlled vehicles to guarantee freshness upon arrival.

General Cargo & Freight

Beyond food, we provide reliable transport services for a wide range of general cargo, supporting businesses across various industries with their distribution needs throughout Sweden.

Distribution & Logistics Partnership

We work as a logistics partner for wholesalers and retailers, offering tailored route planning, scheduled deliveries, and dedicated support to streamline your supply chain.
