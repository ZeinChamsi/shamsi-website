About Mobakher

Mr.Salim started working in olive and olive oil industry as a second job to support his family. In 1975 olive making became his main job and source of income. IIn the 1980 Mubakher became an establishment and started producing olive oil as well. Today, the products have expanded to include stuffed eggplant, pickles, artichoke.. etc. And the area of the factory in Damascus is equal to ten thousand square meters. During the last ten years, a factory was opened in Egypt to expand our coverage areas which includes Egypt, the Gulf, Europe, Canada, and Australia.
